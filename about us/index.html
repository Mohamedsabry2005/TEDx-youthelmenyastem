About TEDx French University

TEDx UFE is an independently organized TED event hosted at the French University in Egypt. We bring together leading thinkers, innovators, and passionate individuals from various fields to share their ideas and stories.

Learn more

Mission

Our Mission is to illuminate hidden ideas, innovate, and enlighten, driving meaningful change, while fostering a community where every individual enriches and motivates the other with their worth spreading ideas.

Vision

Our vision is to become a leading platform for transformative ideas, creating an environment where creativity, learning, and growth thrive.
